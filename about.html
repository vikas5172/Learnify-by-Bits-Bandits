Who we are

We are Learnify, a fast-growing edtech company that is on a mission to make online learning easy, fun, and rewarding for everyone. We believe that online learning is the best way to acquire new skills, explore new interests, and advance your career.

What we do

We provide an all-in-one solution for finding, taking, and creating online courses. With Learnify, you can:

  • Find and take online courses on any topic, from any device, at any time. You can choose from thousands of courses created by experts and verified by Learnify. You can also earn certificates and badges to showcase your achievements.
  • Create and share your own online courses with our easy-to-use course builder. You can use our rich media library, our interactive quizzes, and our feedback tools to create engaging and effective courses. You can also monetize your courses and reach a global audience with our powerful e-commerce and marketing tools.
  • Join and grow your online learning community with our social features. You can connect with other learners and instructors, exchange ideas and opinions, and support each other in your learning journey. You can also join groups, clubs, and events related to your interests and goals.
